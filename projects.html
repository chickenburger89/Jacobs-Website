Lucky and the Sunken Castle

Unreal Engine 5

May 2024

Play as Lucky, an adventure-seeking cat on the hunt for riches in this 3D platformer. Lucky must navigate through increasingly challenging obstacles and face dangerous enemies who stand in his way. He can expand his repertoire of platforming skills by trading collectibles with various NPCs along the way. Delve into a flooded castle, using its system of valves to pump the water out bit by bit, revealing new secrets and paths as you lower the water level.

This was made using Unreal Engine 5 with a team of myself and five others. I designed and built the first level, and helped with the development of the second and third. Eventually, we want to have a handful of levels that each pose new and interesting platforming challenges.

Avalanche 2

Javascript, HTML

December 2023

Avalanche 2 is a game built using the P5.play Javascript library. Laura and I challenged ourselves to recreate a game we had built before but with improved graphics and gameplay. It features multiple selectable stages, toggleable music, and a persistent high-score list. Recreating the gameplay of the original game was a fairly easy challenge to overcome. We knew roughly how we wanted it to play and feel, and we had learned how to research and utilize a javascript library, so we made use of P5.play. In the last game, we did not use any libraries and so had to program the gameplay from scratch. Using P5.play was not only easier to develop with, but resulted in far technically superior gameplay.
