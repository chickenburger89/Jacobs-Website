My Projects

I have experience working on several levels for my Unreal Engine 5 game, so I an proficient with the software. I also have experience using HTML and Javascript to create a variety of websites and programs. I have a strong basis for user experience design.

Lucky and the Sunken Castle

Play as Lucky, an adventure-seeking cat on the hunt for riches in this 3D platformer. Lucky must navigate through increasingly challenging obstacles and face dangerous enemies who stand in his way. Made in Unreal Engine 5.

Avalanche 2

Avalanche 2 is a physics-based high-score game built using the P5.play Javascript library. It features multiple selectable stages, toggleable music, and a persistent high-score list.

MedExplorer

MedExplorer is a healthcare companion app designed to help educate people on healthcare and get the most out of their plans. We undertook a lot of user research including interviews, surveys, and usability testing to determine the best way to design our app and what features to include.
