About Lucky and the Sunken Castle

This Unreal Engine 5 project is the result of about 15 weeks of work from a team of six in total. I did programming and level design, as well as provided input on other aspects like sound and art design. It was my teamâ€™s first major project in Unreal, and I learned a great deal while doing it.

In the initial stages, all we really knew was that we wanted to create a 3D platformer, for a couple of reasons. The first is that most of our group, myself included, are avid gamers who grew up with games like Super Mario 64, Crash Bandicoot, and Jak and Daxter, so we had a good basis for how a 3D platformer should look and feel. The second reason is that since it was our first time working on a major project in Unreal, choosing to create a platformer would help keep things relatively simple.
